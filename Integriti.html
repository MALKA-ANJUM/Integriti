Popular articles from our blog...

What Contraceptive Method Should I Choose?

Contraceptives are designed to prevent pregnancy. They work by stopping the male sperm from reaching the female’s egg. There is a variety of contraceptive methods available and they all work slightly differently.

How To Understand Period Pain

Many women experience an ache in the lower abdomen, back and tops of their legs, especially in the first few days of their period. The first couple of days are usually the worst and then it lessens.

Menopause Management Options

Menopause management is best tailored to each individual. You may not want anything other than the confirmation that your symptoms are menopause related. Without management, the symptoms we’ve discussed can last a few years in most women.

Our fees

At Integriti, our network of services are designed to be accessible and inclusive.

We offer a host of specialist services to women in-house and, if you have a Medicare card, you may be eligible for rebates.

Online Consultations Available.

If you are struggling to see us in person, we can facilitate online video consultations. These consultations can be arranged by contacting the preferred clinician via their booking profile.

Please note that video consultations may not attract Medicare rebates. We recommend that you check that the Integriti GP you select provides online consultations via the booking link or contact us to discuss further.
